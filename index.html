Who is Marvin?

Marvin Lewis was born in Riverside 1992 and raised in Fontana, California. From an early age he displayed signs of being different. At the age of 10 he started acting on television shows and movies, including the Bernie Mac Show and That 70s Show. In middle and high school he went on to play sports and started making music. After highschool he attended California State Polytechnic University, Pomona. He then went on to start a truck driving company and continued trucking for 9 years.
